# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, ethnicity, disability, or background.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Contact

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.
